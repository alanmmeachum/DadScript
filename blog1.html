DadScript: The Origin Story

Allow me to introduce myself, my name is Alan, and I'm embarking on a coding journey – a narrative that began in 1995, the year I was born. But let's fast forward a bit...

In my quest for professional fulfillment, I traversed various industries, some successful, some not so much. Eventually, I found myself drawn to the world of coding, with a desire not only to write code but also to thrive financially. But that's just a part of my story; there's more to it.

Let's rewind to the beginning of my career journey. At 18, I hesitantly dipped my toes into college life, only to withdraw shortly thereafter. It wasn't about the debt, as I often told myself, but rather the fear of change that held me back. Instead, I found myself at a community college, a decision I now regard as one of my best.

Navigating through life, I stumbled into the role of a satellite technician, a 1099 contracted position. While it had its challenges, it was my first taste of the working world. Despite the ups and downs, I longed for something different - an office job, perhaps. And so, I found myself as a fiber technician, only to be laid off not long after.

My journey took me through various roles, including a stint in sales - a job that failed to ignite any passion within me. Yet, amidst the uncertainty, I found solace in problem-solving, a skill honed during my technician days. Despite my interest in software development, I initially doubted my abilities. Nonetheless, I dabbled in Udemy courses, and with each lesson, my confidence grew. But I continued to veer off course, bouncing between jobs in search of fulfillment.

In 2021, I made a pivotal decision to pursue a degree in Computer Science. Opting for an online program, I sought to conquer my fears and engage with the academic community. As I near completion of my degree, I realize there's still much to learn – practical knowledge that transcends textbooks. To bridge this gap, I enrolled in a coding bootcamp, eager to gain hands-on experience. Inspired by my educational journey, I decided to share my experiences through this blog. Beyond coding, I hope to inspire others to persevere in the face of adversity and to find purpose in their pursuits.

So here we are, embarking on this journey together. Through this blog, I aim to offer encouragement, insight, and practical wisdom. Whether you're navigating your own career path or seeking inspiration to overcome challenges, I hope my experiences resonate with you. Welcome to the DadScript Origin Story – let's navigate this journey together, one line of code at a time.
